About this directory

Why I document trips

Planning a trip from scratch is time-consuming. I built this directory so that family, friends, and anyone else can use these itineraries as a starting point — with the research, timing, and practical details already done.

Every itinerary here is one we've actually done (or are actively planning). The timing is real, the restaurant picks are honest, and the accessibility notes are based on actual conditions.

Feel free to copy, adapt, and make these your own.

🕐 Timed, not vague
Every stop has a real time, realistic driving duration, and how long to budget. No "spend some time" hand-waving.
♿ Accessibility-first
Every stop is assessed for mobility considerations, step counts, and alternatives for travellers with knee or joint issues.
🍽️ Food is part of the plan
Restaurant picks, meal timings, and dietary notes are built into the schedule — not afterthoughts.
📋 Book-before-you-go lists
Each trip includes what needs to be reserved weeks in advance — so you don't arrive and miss the highlight.

Open source travel plans

All itineraries live on GitHub — view the source, suggest improvements, or fork for your own trip.
